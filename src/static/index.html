Pending — what this means

Your transaction has been broadcast and is sitting in the mempool — a waiting room that every node keeps. It is valid and visible to the whole network, but no miner has bundled it into a block yet.

How long? Testnet4 produces a block roughly every 10 minutes. With a 1 sat/vByte fee your transaction is typically picked up within 1–3 blocks (10–30 min). This page checks every 5 seconds and will update automatically.

✓ Confirmed — what this means

A miner included your transaction in a block and that block was added to the chain. The block number shown is its permanent position in the blockchain. Your coins are now spendable.

Signature Malleability Demo

ECDSA signatures contain an (r, s) pair. Flipping s → N - s produces a different valid signature — and a completely different TXID — even though the transaction is identical. This is why SegWit was invented.
